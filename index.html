About

Born in LA, shaped by Chicago, finishing up school in Austin. Motion designer and 3D artist — self-taught through YouTube and a love for anything visual.

My work spans motion graphics, 3D visualization, concept design, and live event visuals. I've created visuals for DJ sets, conceptualized campaigns for brands like Under Armour and Nike, and designed drone shows for festivals with Verge Aero.

I'm not precious about style — I can go dark and gritty or clean and commercial. I work fast, solve problems visually, and I'm always pushing into new tools: Cinema 4D, After Effects, Blender, AI-assisted workflows.

Off-screen, I play goalie for the Texas Longhorns hockey team — same problem-solving instinct, just with a stick instead of a stylus.
