Concept Animation — 2024

Nike Factory

A 30-second animation that reimagines the birthplace of Nike — the raw energy and innovation behind the scenes. The iconic logo comes to life, powered by electricity that surges through a futuristic production line.

Inspired by my creative interpretation of Nike's headquarters in Eugene, Oregon, this visual spectacle symbolizes the relentless innovation and cutting-edge technology that drive the brand forward.

Whether as a potential new creative space, a next-gen manufacturing hub, or an inside look at the unseen forces shaping the future of sportswear, Nike Factory represents more than a place — it's the spark of athletic excellence.
