Drone Show Visuals — 2024 / 2025

Verge Aero

At Verge Aero, I designed aerial choreography for major festivals, sporting events, and stadium spectacles across the US and beyond. I worked on visual content design, drone formation choreography, and the technical pipeline that turned creative ideas into synchronized fleets in the sky — from a Longhorn rendered in burnt orange over UT Austin to massive metal logos for Wacken Air in Germany.

One of my strengths is the ability to learn new software quickly. Leveraging my proficiency in other creative and technical programs, I adapted to Verge Aero's custom software seamlessly, optimizing my workflow and contributing to the team's success in a short time.
