Drone Visuals — Summer 2024

Verge Aero

During my summer at Verge Aero, I worked with cutting-edge drone technology, creating breathtaking aerial visuals for major festivals and events. Two standouts: designing visuals for Green Day's electrifying show in San Francisco, and contributing to the drone displays at Wacken Air Festival in Germany — one of the world's biggest metal music festivals.

One of my strengths is the ability to learn new software quickly and intuitively. Leveraging my proficiency in other creative and technical programs, I adapted to Verge Aero's custom software seamlessly, optimizing my workflow and contributing to the team's success in a short time.
