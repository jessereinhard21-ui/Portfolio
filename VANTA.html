3D Motion — 2024

Vanta

A collaboration with a local Austin DJ, crafting dynamic visuals tailored to diverse events and shows. This partnership pushes the boundaries of abstract design in live performances, letting me experiment with new concepts at scale.

Built in Cinema 4D with post-processing in After Effects. The Vanta series explores dark, textured 3D forms — the goal was a visual language that feels both futuristic and organic.

Alongside the motion work, I designed custom text animations that complement the music's energy, and had the opportunity to VJ live at one of the events.
