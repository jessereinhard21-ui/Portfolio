DJ Visuals — 2024

Scotty

I collaborate with a local Chicago DJ, crafting dynamic visuals tailored to his diverse events and shows. This partnership allows me to experiment with new concepts, pushing the boundaries of abstract design in live performances.

Recently, I've been integrating AI-driven visuals into my workflow by using a diffuser plugin to pull Hugging Face models into TouchDesigner, blending them with content I create in Cinema 4D and Adobe CC. This fusion of AI and motion graphics enables unique, evolving visuals that enhance each show's atmosphere.

Beyond designing visuals, I've also developed a passion for typography in motion, crafting custom text animations that complement the music's energy. I had the exciting opportunity to VJ one of his events, bringing my designs to life in real time.
